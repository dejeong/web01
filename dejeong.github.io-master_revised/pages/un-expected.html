Creative direction

2018

We have long for beauty and created it at the beginning of humankind. When it comes to beauty, it is hard to define and describe how we feel about beauty. As a designer and artist, we are always looking for new beauty and trying to create it in our daily life. However, beauty is everywhere in our lives although we want to create the strangeness and new. Sometimes, we look daily objects very different in unusual context, and it is a new kind of pleasure.

When I started this project, I was struggling to get out of boredom of my works which are very similar. It made me get sick of creating something new. One day, I looked at my stuff on my desk, catching the light of the sunset and they seemed amazingly beautiful. There are just a coffee cup, some left-over bread and stationaries. Iʼve never expected they could be beautiful. It happens sometimes in our daily life.

I started to look into materialism and think the meaning of objects if they donʼt have any functions and usage. In this sense, I intent to present the everydayʼs beauty through photography. This is a series of photography that celebrates the pure materiality and simplicity in daily objects. Stepping away from the function, each scultures phogographed were made by the objects soley from an aesthetic point of view.
